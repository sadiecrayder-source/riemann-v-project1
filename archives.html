Historical Repository

Did you know? Leibniz used a vertical bar notation for integration before the stylized 'S' was finalized. He and Newton fought for decades over who actually "owned" the concept of the derivative.

Lore Expansion

  • Archimedes: Used polygons to find the area of a circle (Method of Exhaustion).
  • Cauchy: First to define the integral using limits properly.
  • Leibniz: Invented the \(dx\) notation.
